What We Do

Bereavement Support

Provide comfort and bereavement support through common understanding and shared experience by partnering survivors and those who have lost loved ones in air disasters with trained Volunteer Grief Mentors who have been through a similar experience.

Training

Provide sensitivity training presentations and workshops to companies, agencies and airlines who work with those affected by aviation disasters.

Consulting Services

Provide consulting services to companies and airlines that respond to individuals affected by air disasters

Richard
